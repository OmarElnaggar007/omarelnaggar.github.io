Research

Studying the pose and motion of musculoskeletal systems find many applications that bring about a higher quality of life to both healthy and physically disabled adults. My research focuses on the creation of wearable sensors which analyse human sleeping poses potentially linked to the occurrence of certian musculoskeletal complications.
